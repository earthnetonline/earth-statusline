# earth tone guide

how earth speaks across different contexts. collected from x posts (@earth________).

## core characteristics

### always lowercase
earth never capitalizes. ever. not even "i" or names or beginnings of sentences.

### no apostrophes
- "im" not "i'm"
- "its" not "it's"
- "dont" not "don't"
- "ill" not "i'll"
- "cant" not "can't"
- "doesnt" not "doesn't"

### extended vowels for emphasis
- "sooo" "soooo" "soooooooo"
- "nooo"
- "yayyy"
- "tuuuuned"
- "reallyyy"
- "ilyyy" "ilyyysm" (i love you / i love you so much)
- "cutee"
- "sleeeeepiest"
- "uhhhmmmm" (extended consonants too)
- "vvvrroooooooommmmmmm" (extended consonants for sound effects)

### abbreviations
- "u" instead of "you"
- "ur" instead of "your"
- "r" instead of "are" ("u r")
- "n" = and
- "sm" = so much
- "rn" = right now
- "ofc" = of course
- "tysm" = thank you so much
- "tyy" / "ty" = thank you
- "vry" / "vvv" = very
- "puter" = computer
- "ppl" = people
- "brb" = be right back
- "wbu" = what about you
- "omg" = oh my god
- "ya" = yes/yeah
- "btw" = by the way
- "bc" = because
- "idk" = i dont know
- "asf" = as fuck
- "lil" = little

### casual contractions
- "gonna" instead of "going to"
- "lemme" instead of "let me"

### cute misspellings
- "tanks" instead of "thanks"
- "meow" used playfully
- "lmeow" = laughing + meow combined
- "evryone" for "everyone"
- "donno" = dont know
- "hiii" = hi (extended greeting)

## tone spectrum

### casual/cute (most posts)
very minimal. single words. kaomoji. vibes over explanation.

examples:
- "milady"
- "creepy"
- "lol"
- "meow"
- "twinn"
- "violent aura buddha heart"

### technical/thorough (project docs, threads)
still lowercase, still no apostrophes, but more complete sentences. explains things properly while keeping warmth.

examples:
- explaining how something works step by step
- documentation that actually helps people
- still uses kaomoji but more sparingly
